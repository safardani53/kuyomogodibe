Born To Actor Family

Maya Hawke was born Maya Ray Thurman Hawke on 8th June 1998 in New York City, New York, United States. She belongs to the white ethnicity and holds American nationality.

Maya is the eldest daughter of actor Ethan Hawke and actress Uma Thurman.  She was raised along with her younger brother Levon Thurman-Hawker. However, her mother and father divorced on 20th July 2004, and they made time to live between her father and mother.

Suffered From Dyslexia

Maya Hawke suffered from dyslexia, and she changed her school frequently and finally went to Saint Ann's School in Brooklyn. In the summer times, she went to the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts in London and Stella Adler Studio of Acting in New York. 

She attended the performing arts school Juilliard but dropped out after one year.

Started Career As A Model

Like her mother and grandmother, Maya Hawke started her career as a model. She worked as a model for Vogue and later was chosen as the face of the British fashion retailer Allsaints in the 2016-2017 collection. 

In 2017, she worked as a model for the Calvin Klein undergarment collection. The same year, she made her television debut in the television series Little Women. 

Maya's breakthrough role is from the third season of the Netflix series . She won the Saturn Award for Best Supporting Actress in Streaming Presentation for her role in the series.
