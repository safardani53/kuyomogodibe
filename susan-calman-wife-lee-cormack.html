Who is Susan Calman's wife Lee Cormack?

July 2024 · 2 minute read

FROM being a lawyer, to comedian, to Strictly star - Susan Calman has done it all.

But when she is not busy conquering the world of comedy, she enjoys a cosy homelife with her wife Lee Cormack.

Who is Lee Cormack?

Lee likes to keep a low profile and often steers away from the spotlight, and little is known about her, even her birthday,

Lee has held down a reputable career as a lawyer for over a decade.

Throughout her time with Susan Calman, she has stated multiple times fame doesn't interest her.

It is reported she works as a legal manager for Glasgow City Council.

How did she meet Susan Calman?

Susan first met her wife, Lee in 2002 when they were both lawyers.

They became civil partners in 2012.

What is Susan doing now?

In August 2022, the comedian returned to TV with a second series of her show Susan Calman’s Grand Week by the Sea.

The show is running from August 1 until August 5, 2022 on Channel 5.

The mini-series sees Susan take different celebrities to different coastal towns in the UK.
