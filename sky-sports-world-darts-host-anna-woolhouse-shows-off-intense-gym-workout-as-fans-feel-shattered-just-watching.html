Sky Sports World Darts host Anna Woolhouse shows off intense gym workout as fans 'feel shattered jus

March 2024 · 3 minute read

ANNA WOOLHOUSE is no stranger to a gruelling gym workout.

And footage of her training sessions this year have left her legion of social media followers huffing and puffing.

The Sky Sports Boxing and Darts presenter shared a compilation video of her 2023 workouts with her 157,000 Instagram followers on Thursday.

And it was accompanied by an inspirational message for those looking to start their fitness journeys in the New Year.

She wrote: "In a couple of days there will be a lot of ‘New Year New You’ flying around and a lot of focus on getting fit after overindulging over Christmas.

"Don’t put too much pressure on yourself, there isn’t any quick fix to losing weight or getting fit, it’s all about consistency and most importantly finding something you enjoy whatever that may be!

"I cannot wait to get back into strength training again after a bit of time out due to health stuff.

"Lifting weights and strength training helps you build a strong body and mind too, no ladies it won’t make you bulk up I promise!

"Start steady, be consistent and most importantly enjoy it. Exercise is bloody awesome."

Woolhouse's inspirational message was well received by her followers, although many of them ended up commenting on the gruelling nature of her workouts.

“In the last 48 hours, I have received some awful comments regarding the way I look, being a woman in the industry and about my family which have been hard to ignore, so I decided to highlight this by publicly publishing the messages.
