Celebrities Love Camel Coats: How to Style It Like the Stars

January 2024 · 2 minute read

There are three things you need to master fall 2015: a cute sweater, a Pumpkin Spice Latte (or 20), and, if you’re following the stars, a fierce camel-colored coat.

A-listers have been slaying the season in the autumnal-hued topper, rocking it with everything from a black mini (a la Jennifer Lawrence) to jeans and sneakers (like Gigi Hadid), to a glitter-bombed black-tie dress (we see you, Kate Hudson!).

And why does it work so well? As the neutral of the season, there’s nothing that won’t match the hue. Plus, camel coats tend to boast a crisp cut, making them a sophisticated addition to any ensemble.

Scroll down for more celeb-modeled camel coatspiration!

Ahead of the Mockingjay – Part 2 world premiere, the Hunger Games actress, 25, stepped out in Berlin rocking a camel Michael Kors Collection coat with a black minidress and over-the-knee boots on Nov. 1.

Renee Zellweger hit the carpet at the SeriousFun Children’s Network Gala 2015 in London on Nov. 3, wearing her cafe au lait trench as a dress, teamed with embellished pumps.

Serving some ’70s vibes, Hadid, 20, wore a suede coat over a white crop top, flared Frame jeans, and a top-handle purse on Oct. 6 in Paris.

Doubled up! Nicole Scherzinger draped a light camel coat over a royal blue suit while out in NYC on Oct. 20.

While visiting The Late Show With Stephen Colbert on Oct. 26, Sienna Miller bundled up in a sandy Rag & Bone topper, teamed with a strappy black dress and stacked heels.

Meow! Demi Lovato sizzled in the same Rag & Bone coat, an olive-green crop top and skirt, both by Alice + Olivia, and leopard-print accessories on Oct. 15 in NYC.

Making the coat the whole focus, Hilary Swank attended the Tokyo International Film Festival 2015 on Oct. 23, wearing a double-breasted Balmain dress in nude velvet, paired with cross-strap Christian Louboutin heels.
