Who was rapper Lil Loaded and how did he die? 

Dashawn Maurice Robertson, aka Lil Loaded, passed away on Monday, May 31 at the age of 20.

His cause of death is still unknown.

According to CBS46, the rapper had allegedly shared a cryptic message on his Instagram prior to his death asking God for forgiveness. 

The post has since been deleted.

Born in California on August 1, 2000, the rapper moved to Dallas, Texas, with his family when he was young.

He took up rapping in 2018 and came to prominence when YouTuber Tommy Craze posted a reaction video about his song Blocc Baby.

Loaded was best known for his songs 6locc 6a6y, Gang Unit, and Link Up.

Loaded released an album - A Demon In Blue - in October 2020, which peaked at No19 on the Top Heatseekers chart.

Why was Lil Loaded in jail?

Loaded was accused of allegedly shooting teenager Khalil Walker with a rifle outside of his home in October 2020.

The teenager had been shot in the torso and was rushed to the Methodist Charlton Medical Centre, but died from his injuries.

Loaded was arrested on a murder charge, but in March 2021 was indicted on a lesser one, manslaughter.
