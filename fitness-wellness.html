How does yoga sound to you? Whether you've tried it before or not, people hold reservations when it comes to yoga because they don't follow the proper guidelines. Plus, they don't provide their body with the support it needs to become flexible and easy-going. What you need, then, is a yoga strap to make your troubles go away.

Finding the right yoga strap might not be easy. The market is crawling with different products that may leave you with more questions. Instead, we'll recommend you follow through with this article and get to know the necessary tips and tricks to buy the perfect product for your next yoga session.

What You Should Look Out For When Buying Yoga Straps

Price

Price is something that is a given. You won't buy a yoga buckle that's worth a fortune, right? Just make sure you don't compromise too much on the price. Because mostly, price and quality go hand in hand. Keep that in mind when you're making a purchase.

Durability

From the get-go, your priority should be durability. If the yoga buckle is durable, then you can tick off most of your worries from the list. This is because yoga asks for a lot of stretches and you will need to flex your body countless times. In case the strap is not durable, you will always be afraid of bending or stretching too much, and that's not how you practice peacefulness, right?

Another element that is directly connected to durability is strength. The strength of the buckle should be thick so that it can help with wear and tear.

Size

The yoga buckle you are buying should be of an appropriate length. When doing yoga, you will need to make sure that the length of the strap is enough to let you move freely, without restricting your movements or making you uncomfortable. However, that doesn’t mean you should go to higher lengths unnecessarily. In case you are tall, or if you want to try a different size then change the length.

Buckle

For yoga practices, buckle straps are extremely common. This is because they can increase your flexibility and you can adjust their width according to the practice you are doing or your body type.

Cleaning and maintenance

We don’t want to throw you off here so we’ll make one thing clear: most yoga buckles don’t ask for a lot of care and maintenance. You can leave your cotton ones in the machine and after a quick wash, they’ll look brand new. In case you don’t have a machine, just let them rest in soapy water and after a while, hang them somewhere so that they dry. However, this does not apply to all of them. Some are made of sensitive materials that don’t respond well to soaking. Make sure you ask the seller or check the packaging of the product before washing it.

Eco-friendly materials

Something popular these days is eco-friendly products. If your strap is made from harmful materials that can not only harm the environment but you as well. Don't worry, we'll get to the materials next. First and foremost, you want to avoid all the products that use dyes. Secondly, steer clear from the ones that use PVC materials in them.

Materials

Speaking of materials, you are free to choose whichever fabric you want but don't forget one thing: there's nothing that beats cotton. Because cotton webbing fabric will greatly increase its strength and durability, both of these elements are essential. Cotton is known to be durable and convenient when it comes to cleaning. However, some people dislike cotton straps because they feel that it is too rough. That may hold for people who have sensitive skin. Otherwise, cotton straps are good to go.

In case you have sensitive skin, go for organic cotton because it is much softer. You can also find buckles made of nylon and rubber as well. Whichever material you opt for, just make sure your skin isn’t allergic to it. Yogis prefer rubber these days because it offers much more elasticity and it isn’t as stiff as cotton or nylon. Of course, your choice will also be affected by your personal preferences.

Benefits of Wearing Yoga Straps

If worn the right way, yoga buckles can greatly help you. Starting with something that you want to avoid at all costs, will help save you from potential injuries. Perhaps one of the biggest misconceptions about yoga is that it is completely safe. Some practices are very difficult to do without a yoga strap. In case you gnaw at them without proper support, you may suffer a serious injury. That’s why we advise against going into hardcore yoga without proper support at all costs.

Secondly, yoga buckles will help to balance your posture. These days, people suffer from all kinds of back-related problems. Their postures are messed up and that’s where yoga straps pitch in. Even experienced yogis cannot complete all the practices without taking the assistance of yoga accessories.

Thirdly, they will reduce the stiffness in your muscles. Do you suffer from stiff shoulders by any chance? No worries, try using yoga buckles and you will feel that with time, your flexibility and muscle stiffness are going away.

Yes, there is always a chance of injuries but if you're wearing yoga buckles, you will feel a lot safer. Another benefit is that after wearing them, your muscles will feel less stiff. Of course, when the quality of your stretches increases you will automatically feel more relieved. This will reduce muscle stiffness.
