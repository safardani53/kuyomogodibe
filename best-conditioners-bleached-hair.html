12 Best Conditioners For Bleached Hair 2021 | The Sun UK

February 2024 · 8 minute read

IF you regularly dye your hair, then regularly using one of the best conditioners for bleached hair is an absolute essential to keep your colour looking fresh.

Bleaching takes its toll on your hair, making it more prone to breakage and split ends and typically giving it an overall rougher, less lustrous appearance.

If you’re thinking of going blonder for the summer months, there are a few things to keep in mind when it comes to hair care.

First off, you can’t just bleach your hair and ignore it - bleached hair requires maintenance both in and out of the salon.

Those going blonder tend to invest in a purple shampoo - there are many versions, from drugstore to designer – available to maintain your colour and tone down any brassiness in between pro visits or at-home dye jobs.

When it comes to finding the best conditioner for bleached hair, there are nourishing ones with natural oils like the Olive Fruit Oil Nourishing Conditioner from Kiehl’s.

But some brands use more complex ingredients to help damaged hair.

For example, Olaplex, a beloved brand for bleaching devotees, has bis-aminopropyl diglycol dimaleate as the core ingredient in its cult haircare products, which works on a molecular level to rebuild damaged hair strands.

The good news is that you can also find conditioners for bleached hair in your favourite supermarket, like Tresemmé’s new Colour Shineplex Cleansing Conditoner, a sulphate-free option which nourishes bleached hair and costs less than a fiver.

The best conditioners for bleached hair ensure your colour looks luminous and your hair stays soft, silky and hydrated. Here's our round-up.
