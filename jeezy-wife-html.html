All You Need to Know About Jeezys Wife Jeannie Mai

May 2024 Â· 3 minute read

American rapper Jeezy and his wife Jeannie Mai are a happy couple. The two got married earlier this year in March, following over two years of dating.

In those two years, Jeezy and Mai took the internet by storm on a few occasions with their stunning couple snaps, attended the inaugural SnoBall Gala in Atlanta together, and popularly appeared on Dancing With the Stars.

They then got engaged, as the rapper surprised Mai with a proposal during a date night at home in Los Angeles in April 2020.

The couple recently announced that they are expecting their first child together, despite Mai previously saying she didn't want children.

That said, who is Jeezy's beloved wife, Mai and what is her profession? Here's everything you need to know about the rapper's wife, including her family and past relationships.

Jeannie Mai Belongs to the Entertainment Industry

Born Jeannie Mai Jenkins, Mai is a 42-year-old makeup artist, fashion expert, actress, and TV personality from San Jose, California. She formerly hosted Style Network's show How Do I Look? and USA Network's show Character Fantasy.

The glamourous diva has also appeared as a fashion expert on television programs, such as The Today, NBC's Extra with Billy Bush, and E! 's The Daily 10.

Jeannie Mai Is of Mixed Ethnicity

Born and raised in San Jose, Mai is the daughter of a Vietnamese immigrant father and Chinese mother, making her roots attached to two different ethnicities.

And it was her mother's footsteps that led her to the world of entertainment and fashion. She began performing at an early age, using her mother's makeup to shine herself up before leaping out to "interview" her 16 family members living in her three-bedroom home.

Previously Married to Freddy Harteis

Before her relationship with her now-husband Jeezy, The Real co-host was married to Freddy Harteis, a professional hunter, outdoorsman, and television producer. The ex-couple were married to each other between 2007 and 2018.

The marriage ended in divorce, with the couple citing irreconcilable differences as the reason for the split. The divorce was finalized in January 2019.

"To this day, I mean it honestly, he's the best man I know...I married him because he's like my dad," Mai said at the time of divorce publicly. "He's very loyal, he's very compassionate, so funny, an awesome guy and in our 13 years of being together there was never any lies, no betrayal, no cheating, nothing."

Jeannie Mai Actively Contributes to the Society

Besides being a talented performer and makeup artist, Mai is also a philanthropist who annually travels to places like Africa, Thailand, and Vietnam to spread social awareness.

During her visits, she works closely with organizations like Heartbeat Vietnam, a nonprofit devoted to improving health care for impoverished children, and NightLight International, an organization committed to rescuing women and children from sex trafficking and prostitution rings by providing employment and vocational opportunities.

In addition, she is committed to supporting females who face obstacles in their careers by partnering with organizations like Seven Bar, Dress for Success, Sister To Sister, P.A.T.H, Respect RX, and The Good Shepherd.
