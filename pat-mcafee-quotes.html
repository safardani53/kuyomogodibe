Top 69 Pat McAfee Quotes

June 2024 · 10 minute read

Patrick Justin McAfee is the full name of one of the highly renowned sports analyst in America, podcaster, professional wrestler, and a former punter. But he is known by his short name, Pat McAfee. Currently, he signed to WWE and performed on the NXT brand. Pat made two Pro Bowls through his 8-years of career in the National Football League (NFL) and was an All-Pro in 2014.

Moreover, he was retired in February 2017 from football and worked as an analyst in football and professional wrestling. He was also a guest host for Fox Sports College and NFL broadcasts in late 2018. And later, he was announced as part of ESPN’s Thursday Night College Football team in July 2019. In 2019, he signed the next contract in February 2019, but just before that, in 2018, he guested for EEW’s NXT TakeOver events.

Pat McAfee is a multi-talent person as he is also recognized as a stand-up comedian and is presented in numerous events around Indiana. Now, he is running his daily podcast show.
