Jennifer Lopez's new perfume smells just like Le Labo's 200 Santal 33

April 2024 · 3 minute read

ANYTHING Jennifer Lopez - aka JLo - wears, buys, or does, we are obsessed, whether it's a new single, movie, documentary, fashion or beauty drop, we are over it.

Lo and behold, JLo has launched a brand new fragrance, titled One, and the scent may be familiar to some.

  • Jennifer Lopez One Eau de Parfum, £25 from Superdrug - buy here

Jennifer Lopez One perfume retails for £25 at Superdrug, though it is also available to shop from Boots, The Perfume Shop, and Amazon, plus more retailers.

The launch of the One Eau De Parfum comes after the success of her Promise fragrance.

The One is a new scent and has been described as having notes of warm and spicy pink pepper, and suede, combined with soft florals, such as jasmine, earthy layers of wood, moss and patchouli to avoid it being a sickly sweet scent.

However, some have noted it smells very similar to the iconic Le Labo scent, Santal 33, but a fraction of the price.

The Ain't Your Mama hitmaker's fragrances ranges in price depending on the retailer, and size of the bottle.

At Superdrug it fetches £25, whereas Santal 33 can span anywhere from £66 to £808.

Even the smallest 15ml sample of Santal 33 is almost three times more expensive than a 30ml One scent.

Most read in Fabulous

Woman & Home has claimed the 53-year-old singer and actor has a penchant for Le Labo, in particular Santal 26, which is a sweeter variation of the hugely popular scent from the brand.

With that in mind, some believe JLo has taken inspiration from the label for her own creations, although she has yet to reveal the source of her inspiration for the scent.

What we do know is JLo's One fragrance aims to unite shoppers.

The product description reads: "ONE is a feeling, an energy, a way of being that reflects harmony and brings peace. ONE is internal and eternal. It is not limited, but it is expansive, vulnerable, powerful. Today our world needs ONE-ness, realising we are ONE world and ONE people. Living for ONE shared hope, ONE bigger meaning and purpose. ONE goal. ONE dream. Connected, not separate, we are ONE."

Santal 33 also boasts floral and smokey, woody notes, as it combines cedarwood, with leather, sandalwood, and soft florals, including violet accord and iris.

Le Labo ranges from £66 for a small 15ml, £220 for 100ml, or £808 for a whopping 500ml bottle.

Admittedly Le Labo's scents are longlasting, and a little spritz goes a long way.

But, if you can save anywhere from £41, up to £780, why wouldn't you?
