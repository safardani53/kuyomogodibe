What is a Thumb Thumb from Spy Kids?

THE FIRST Spy Kids movie premiered in 2001 starring Alexa Vega and Daryl Sabara.

On March 30, 2022, Netflix announced the relaunch of the Spy Kids franchise alongside original filmmaker Robert Rodriguez.

What is a Thumb Thumb from Spy Kids?

In the original Spy Kids franchise, a TV host named Fegan Floop and his second-in-command, Alexander Minion, work to develop an army of robot children.

Thumb Thumbs are giant robot henchmen made entirely of thumbs - their limbs and head included.

Throughout the series, the clumsy and notorious Thumb Thumbs continuously make appearances.

What is the Spy Kids reboot?

Who was in the original Spy Kids?

The original Spy Kids movies featured the main cast of Alexa Vega as Carmen Cortez, Daryl Sabara as Juni Cortez, Antonio Banderas as Gregorio Cortez, and Carla Gugino as Ingrid Cortez.

Danny Trejo appeared in the films as Gregorio's brother, Isador.

Fegan Floop took life in Alan Cumming, with Tony Shalhoub playing Alexander Minion.

An animated television spin-off was released in 2018, hitting Netflix with a total of 20 episodes.
