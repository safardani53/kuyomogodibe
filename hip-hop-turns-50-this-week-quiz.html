Hip hop turns 50 this week - but are you a rapper's delight? Find out with our quiz

August 2024 · 3 minute read

FEATURING big bling, bigger beats and even bigger personalities – hip-hop is a music genre unlike any other.

The revolutionary style celebrated its 50th birthday this week, which is being marked with massive events across the globe.

At age 18, DJ Kool Herc – real name Clive Campbell – founded the genre with two turntables and a microphone at a party in the Bronx, New York, in 1973.

It has gone on to influence everything from politics to fashion.

To mark hip-hop’s birthday take the ultimate quiz put together by Josh Saunders.

a) Future; b) Kanye West; c) Travis Scott

2) How many times has 50 Cent – aka Curtis James Jackson III – been shot?

a) Two; b) Four; c) Nine
