Victoria Beckham flogs 590 jumper which is near-identical to West Ham footie kit The Sun

September 2024 · 2 minute read

VICTORIA Beckham is flogging a claret and blue jumper which is almost identical to West Ham's football kit.

The former Spice Girl is selling the top, which is made of alpaca wool blend, for £590.

Posh, 44, describes the knit as “ideal for pairing with high waist skirts and trousers,” but it wouldn’t look out of place at the Hammers’ home in the London stadium.

Victoria isn’t a fan of the beautiful game and previously admitted to paying little attention to her husband David’s profesion during his national and international career.

When asked on her thoughts about football, Victoria said: “I call what David does football competitions.

“I can never remember what they’re supposed to be. Football games?”

Posh is linked to West Ham through her husband David, 44, whose local team were West Ham when he was growing up in Leytonstone.

The Hammers previously failed to tempt David into playing for them when his stint at LA Galaxy ended in 2010.

Club chairman David Gold said at the time: “It would be a fantastic statement if he came to West Ham.

“He’s an East End boy and it would be a fitting end to his career.”

It’s not the first time Posh’s designer clobber has raised eyebrows.

Last year she was called out for her outrageously expensive collaboration with sports brand Reebok, who were selling near-identical items for a fraction of the cost.
