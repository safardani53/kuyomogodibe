‘A Cinderella Story: Christmas Wish’ is now available on Digital and will be available on Blu-ray and DVD on Oct. 29. The movie is the fifth installment in the ‘A Cinderella Story’ franchise. This film is a contemporary musical that follows Katherine “Kat” Decker (Laura Marano), who dreams of becoming a famous singer-songwriter. Her reality, however, is suffocated by her conniving and cruel stepfamily and a job working as a singing elf at billionaire Terrence Wintergarden’s Santa Land. When she starts to fall for Nick (Gregg Sulkin), the handsome new Santa at the tree lot, things begin to look up. But when her stepmother and stepsisters realize Kat has been invited to the Wintergarden Christmas Gala, they’ll stop at nothing to get their own invitation and keep Kat from attending. With the help of her attentive dog, a true friend, and a little bit of holiday magic, Kat may just make it to the gala after all, discover the true identity of Nick, and experience the true meaning of Christmas.
