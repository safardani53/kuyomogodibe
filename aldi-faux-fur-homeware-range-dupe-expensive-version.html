Aldis new homeware line has faux fur rugs and stools from 9 &amp; its a dupe of this 295 versi

August 2024 · 4 minute read

WE’RE all going to be spending more time at home as winter kicks in and the UK continues to battle the pandemic, so you want to make it as cosy as possible. 

Aldi has got you covered with their new homeware range, featuring luxurious faux fur throws, cushions, rugs and stools - from £9. 

The collection lands in-store on October 11, but you can pre-order your favourite pieces online now. 

The range, part of Aldi’s SpecialBuys, is sure to add a chic flare to your home, and it’s a fraction of the cost of designer versions, with similar items on sale for nearly £300. 

The fluffy stools are arguably the stand-out goodies from Aldi, with a round white one, a round grey one and square grey one all costing £14.99. 

Describing the seats, Aldi said shoppers can add a “ touch of luxury” to their home. 

  • Round White Long Faux Fur Stool, Aldi, £14.99 - buy now
  • Tibetan Sheepskin Stool, The White Company, £295 - buy now

The website says: “Super soft and comfortable, this lovely shaggy stool will add a touch of glamour to your room while also giving you a cosy place to rest.”

The price-tag is an absolute steal when compared to a near-identical version from The White Company, which their stool setting you back nearly £300.

While the grey one is  bargain when compared to House of Isabella’s offering, which will set you back £186.25. 

  • Round Grey Short Faux Fur Stool, Aldi, £14.99 - buy now
  • Grey sheepskin stool, House of Isabella, £186.25 - buy now

If it’s rugs you’re after for the cold winter nights ahead, Aldi is selling a set in white, brown, dark and light grey. 

Each one costs £9.99, and Aldi described them as: “Durable with a faux suede backing, this rug is both stylish and practical, making it a great addition to any lounge, hall or bedroom.”

You could pick up the entire range for less than £50, which is still a fraction of the cost of Ugg’s similar versions, in sand, grey and natural - costing a whopping £120 a pop. 

  • Kirkton House Faux Fur Rug, Aldi, £9.99 - buy now
  • Sheepskin rug, Ugg, £120 - buy now

No night in front of the telly is complete without a cosy throw, and Aldi’s are flying off the shelves at just £34.99 each, with three shades to choose from in white, dark and light grey. 

Aldi says the machine-washable throw with a plush reverse is ideal for “snuggling up on the sofa during those cold evenings”, and we agree. 

The White Company also has a range of similar shades and various sizes - but theirs will set you back £200 each from Selfridge's. 

  • Kirkton House Faux Fur Throw, Aldi, £34.99 - buy now
  • The White Company Super Soft Faux-Fur Throw, Selfridge's £200 - buy now

The company also sells faux fur cushions as part of their own range, which may be soft to the touch, but at £40 you’d never want anything to spill on them. 

The expensive versions come in dove grey, alabaster and charcoal, just like Aldi’s trio which costs just £8.99 each. 

Aldi’s bargain cushions are double sided, with the supermarket claiming they give off a ‘hotel chic’ vibe.

  • Kirkton House Faux Fur Cushion, Aldi, £8.99 - buy now
  • Super Soft Faux-Fur Cushion Cover, The White Company, £40 - buy now
