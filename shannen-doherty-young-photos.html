See all of the best photos of Shannen Doherty from her younger years to today.

Shannen Doherty was born on April 12, 1971 & she is an American actress, producer author, and television director. Shannen started off her career at 11 years old as a series regular on Little House on the Prairie and then in 1989, she was cast in her first major motion picture – Heathers. After that she played the iconic role of Brenda Walsh in Beverly Hills, 90210. About eight years later, Shannen starred in the TV show Charmed and she left the series in 2001.
