Lily Anne Harrison And Peter Facinelli Welcomed Their First Child

May 2024 · 3 minute read

Peter Facinelli, an American actor, just had his first child with his girlfriend Lily Anne Harrison. Lily is a 33-year-old actress who has acted in films such as Two Guys One Truck, Fair Haven, and The Year of Spectacular Men. After they were seen together in 2016, she fueled romance speculations with Peter. In 2019, the couple got engaged. On September 5, 2022, Peter Facinelli posted a monochromatic photo of their newborn kid grasping his finger to his Instagram account. He captioned the photo as follows:

“Have a Happy “Labor” Day, @lilyanneharrison, September 5th, 2022.”

While this is Peter’s first child with Lily, he already had three children from his former marriage to Jennie Garth.

All you need to know about Peter Facinelli’s fiancée.

Lily Anne Harrison was born on February 9, 1989, in Los Angeles, California, to actor Gregory Harrison and model Randi Oakes. Lesly Kahn & Company, The Groundlings School of Improv, Ivana Chubbuck Studio, UCLA, and Margie Haber Studio are among the Los Angeles-based institutions where she studied screenplay, acting, and improvisational comedy.

According to her IMDb page, she began her acting career in the CW series One Tree Hill. She has since featured in a number of television shows and films, including The Craft Store, Breaking & Exiting, Good Deed, Christmas Camp, Ruthless Realtor, and others.

Peter and Lily Relationship

Peter Facinelli and Lily Anne Harrison initially ignited relationship rumors in September 2016, shortly after he ended his engagement with Jaimie Alexander. The couple was seen on a date at Studio City’s Pinz Bowling Center.

A source close to the pair told ET at the time:

“It’s obviously new, and they’re having a good time.”

Harrison said at the 2022 RomaDrama that she knew Facinelli was the one after their first date and had contacted her parents shortly after to inform them she had met her spouse. In 2019, the couple became engaged after Peter proposed to them while they were on vacation in Mexico. Peter confessed to People Magazine in 2021 that he and Harrison are not in a haste to complete the wedding rituals. He stated:

“I want [our wedding] to take place in a world without masks. We’re not in a hurry. We are loyal to one another and share a life together. I’m already married. Except for the piece of paper and the celebration, we have everything!”


Prior to Harrison, she was married to Beverly Hills alum Jennie Garth from 2001 until 2013. The former couple has three daughters: Luca (24), Lola (19), and Fiona (16). Lily Anne Harrison confirmed in June 2022 that she was expecting her first child with Peter Facinelli. She posted a selfie with the baby bump on her Instagram account. On September 5, 2022, their child was born. Several friends, fans, and family members praised the parents in the comments section of Peter Facinelli’s Instagram post. Although some of these communications imply that the newborn kid is a male, the parents have yet to confirm this.
