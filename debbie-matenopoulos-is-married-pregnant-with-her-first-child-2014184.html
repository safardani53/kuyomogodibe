Debbie Matenopoulos Is Married, Pregnant With Her First Child

April 2024 · 1 minute read

Surprise — times two! Debbie Matenopoulos is married and pregnant, she confirms to Us Weekly. The Home & Family host secretly tied the knot with her boyfriend Jon Falcone last summer. 

"I feel so blessed that this is happening to me," Matenopoulos, 39, tells Us. "Everything in life is truly beautiful." 

"I eloped in Greece and am four months pregnant," she adds. "It has been a great journey so far."

This will be the second marriage for the It's All Greek to Me cookbook author, who first announced the pair's happy news on her Hallmark Channel series on April 17. She was married to L.A. music executive Jay Faires for five years until their divorce in 2008.
