Heidi Newfield is an American country music artist. She was the lead vocalist, rhythm guitarist and harmonica player for the band Trick Pony, alongside Keith Burns and Ira Dean from 1996 to 2006, when she pursued a solo career. Newfield started his solo career on Curb Records, debuting in 2008 with the single "Johnny & June". The song peaked at number 11 on the Hot Country Songs chart, which was the debut release of her debut solo album, What Am I Waiting For, creating her second country hit in the Top 30, “Cry Cry ".
