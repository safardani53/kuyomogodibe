What is Colin Jost's net worth?

Colin Jost is worth $8million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Jost is an American comedian and writer from New York.

He is known for writing for Saturday Night Live since 2005 and being a co-anchor for Weekend Update since 2014.

After graduating from Harvard, he worked as a reporter and briefly did the writing for a Nickelodeon show.

As a stand-up comedian, he had appeared on Late Night With Jimmy Fallon.

Alongside all this, he's written for The New Yorker, published his own memoir and had minor acting roles.

Does Colin Jost have children?

Rumors started speculating back in July 2021 that him and wife, Scarlett Johansson, were expecting their first child together.

Jost later confirmed the news in August 2021 while performing at a stand-up gig at the Ridgefield Playhouse in Connecticut.

"We’re having a baby, it’s exciting,” Jost told the crowd.

Just days after the news was confirmed, the couple's first child was born.

While the baby's name and gender has yet to be revealed, Johansson's rep, Marcel Pariseau, confirmed the news to People on August 18, 2021.

While this is Jost's first child, this is Johansson's second.

Back in 2014, she gave birth to her daughter Rose Dorothy Dauriac, 6, who is from her previous marriage to Romain Dauriac.

Details of the custody battle that took place over her aren't publicly known as Johansson has made an effort to keep this private, though it looks like the couple share custody.

When did Scarlett Johansson and Colin Jost get married?

Sometime over a weekend in late October 2020, Jost married Hollywood actress Johansson.

They kept the celebrations "intimate" and in line with coronavirus safety rules.

The couple wanted the news to benefit the wider community, so decided to let Meals On Wheels America break the news story on Instagram.

Part of the post reads: "Their wedding wish is to help make a difference for vulnerable older adults during this difficult time by supporting @mealsonwheelsamerica."
