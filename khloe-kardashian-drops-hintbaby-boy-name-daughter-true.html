Khloe Kardashian finally drops hint about baby boy's name &amp; reveals what daughter True, 4, really ca

May 2024 · 4 minute read

KHLOE Kardashian has revealed that her daughter, True Thompson, spilled the latest hint about her son's name, even if it is just a nickname.

Khloé, 38, welcomed her second child via surrogacy in July of this year, but has yet to reveal her son's name publicly.

The arrival of her baby boy was documented in the Season 2 premiere of The Kardashians where she introduced her child to her family via FaceTime.

During a video call with her daughter, True, viewers picked up on the cute nickname the child had given her little brother.

"Hi, Snowy," the four-year-old said as Khloé giggled and replied: "His name is not Snowy."

During an appearance on The Kelly Clarkson Show this week, Khloé was grilled about her son's name - but she still refused to give much away.

"So wait, you haven't revealed your son's name yet, So is there like a family name? A hint?" Kelly asked the reality TV star.

"Well my daughter says his name is Snowy, and it's not Snowy - so that's the hint!" Khloé replied.

Laughing at her comment, Kelly said: "I was like: 'Are you a big Game of Thrones fan!' Jon Snow!"

"Oh my God, I swear she does things just to mess with me but his name is not Snowy!" Khloé reiterated.

Earlier on in the interview, the Good American founder opened up about her surrogacy journey and confirmed she had reasons why she couldn't carry her second child.

Referring to her sister Kim Kardashian and her surrogacies, Khloé said: "Not every journey's the same, mine was very different from hers. I think her journey, I think she was just... it was more comfortable for her.

"For me, I'm such a control freak! I loved my surrogate, she was amazing but I'm still like, 'I need you sitting next to me all the time, I need to know what you're doing, what are you eating'," she continued.

"And a control freak, I take as a compliment, so I'm not offended that I'm a control freak!" Khloé added.

Earlier this month, fans shared a theory on why they think the star has kept her baby son's name a secret.

FANS' THEORY

Many people think that Khloé doesn't want to reveal it because of potential backlash or criticism.

In a recent video from a Halloween party she threw for the kids, Khloé showed off a sweet gift that was sent from sister Kourtney Kardashian.

During the video, the Hulu star showcased the Halloween-themed gingerbread house gifted by Kourtney, 43, and the Lemme founder's husband, Travis Barker, 46.

She recorded her daughter True, five, snacking on candy beside the gift while capturing the miniature home's spooky decorations.

The frosting on the roof displayed the names of the members of Khloe's household.

Both "Khloe" and "True" were written in icing while the family's newest addition simply had the word "baby" filling in for his actual moniker.

But fans, who commented on the Halloween party photos on a popular Kardashian Reddit page, theorized why Khloé is hiding the baby moniker.

One fan questioned: "I wonder why she doesn't want his name out for the pubic to know? Why is she copying Kylie [Jenner] or has she named him Tristan Junior and is too embarrassed to say?"
