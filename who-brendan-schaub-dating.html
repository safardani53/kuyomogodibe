Who is Brendan Schaub dating?

June 2024 · 2 minute read

FORMER UFC fighter Brendan Schaub has been engaged to longtime girlfriend Joanna Zanella.

Schaub was previously linked to fellow UFC fighter Ronda Rousey.

Who is Brendan Schaub dating?

Brendan Schaub has been dating sports host and entrepreneur Joanna Zanella since 2014.

Zanella formerly worked as an anchor for UFC. 

She is reportedly a TV host at Fox Deportes, the first Spanish sports network in the US.

Zanella also starred in the Spanish film Un dia en el banco, which was released in 2009. 

She was also featured in two more movies and one TV series, according to her IMDB page.

Zanella also has her own clothing line called JZ.STYLE.

Schaub and Zanella have two children, Tiger, and Boston Schaub.

Who is Brendan Schaub?

Schaub is an American stand-up comedian, podcast host, and former professional mixed martial artist.

The 38-year-old is the host of The Fighter and the Kid podcast and the BELOW THE BELT with Brendan Schaub podcast.

He also co-hosts the King and the Sting podcast, along with fellow comedian Theo Von.

Schaub retired from MMA in 2015 and since released his first comedy special titled You'd Be Surprised in 2019.

On August 18, 2021, TMZ reported that the former fighter saved four children from a fatal car wreck after their father allegedly drove the wrong way on the highway.

The children’s mom, who was in the passenger seat, was killed in the collision.

According to Schaub, he saw a child sitting on top of the vehicle screaming for help so he pulled over to assist.

Schaub alleged that he saw a shoeless man, presumed to be the father, run from the scene across the highway where he was hit by another vehicle, leaving him on the ground.

According to a police report, the man was not injured and had to be tased prior to being arrested.
