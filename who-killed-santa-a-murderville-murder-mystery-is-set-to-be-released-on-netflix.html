Who Killed Santa? A Murderville Murder Mystery Is Set To Be Released On Netflix

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Who Killed Santa? is a new Christmas special on Netflix. The streaming platform will debut A Murderville Murder Mystery on Thursday, December 15, at 3 a.m. ET. The return of the popular Murderville character Detective Terry Seattle, who has to find out who killed Santa, is featured in the special. Will Arnett will play the primary part in the special, with many others playing crucial supporting roles. Who assassinated Santa Claus? Laura Murphy directs A Murderville Murder Mystery.

Details about the teaser and what to anticipate

On November 18, Netflix released the official teaser for the film, which gives a glimpse of the show’s entertaining and eccentric characters. The video depicts Will Arnett’s character attempting to find out who committed the murder. The first trailer for Who Killed Santa? A Murderville Murder Mystery has some humorous moments while avoiding exposing too many vital information that may ruin fans’ watching experience. Overall, the teaser maintains Murderville’s distinctively wacky tone, which fans of the show will recognize. Along with the teaser, Netflix released a summary for the special, which reads as follows:

“Senior Detective Terry Seattle (Will Arnett) is back, and the case is crucial this time. He’s on a journey with two celebrity guests, Jason Bateman and Maya Rudolph, to uncover…who murdered Santa?”

The description continues:

“But here’s the catch: Bateman and Rudolph aren’t getting a screenplay. They are completely unaware of what is going to happen to them. They will have to improvise their way through the case with Terry Seattle (and many surprises).”

According to the trailer and summary of Who Killed Santa? A Murderville Murder Mystery, fans can anticipate a wonderfully enjoyable and amusing comedy special ideal for Christmas watching as renowned investigator Terry Seattle attempts to probe deep into Santa’s murder. The special is part of Netflix’s popular comedy program, Murderville, which debuted in early 2022 and earned rave reviews for its improvised humor and performances by the cast.

A quick examination of Who Killed Santa? Cast and crew of A Murderville Murder Mystery

Will Arnett reprises his role as Terry Seattle in Who Killed Santa? Murderville is a murder mystery. In the preview for the show, he looks fantastic, exhibiting his distinct sense of humor as he dominates the teaser with his comedic charm. The star is expected to provide another fascinating performance.

Will Arnett is most recognized for his roles in Arrested Development, Blades of Glory, and Let’s Go To Prison, among other things. He also played Bojack Horseman in Netflix’s legendary animated tragicomedy BoJack Horseman. Jason Bateman, Maya Rudolph, and Lilan Bowden, among others, appear in important supporting roles with Will Arnett. Laura Murphy is well recognized for her work on Girl Code, Awkwafina Is Nora from Queens, The Iliza Shlesinger Sketch Show, and other projects.
