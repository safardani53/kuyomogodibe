Are Jayden Bartels and Jules LeBlanc dating?

March 2024 · 3 minute read

YOUTUBERS and Nickelodeon stars Jayden Bartels and Jules LeBlanc rose to fame online, building huge followings from a young age.

The two star alongside each other in the buddy comedy television series, Side Hustle.

Are Jayden Bartels and Jules LeBlanc dating?

While many fans of the two actresses have personally "shipped" them together, Jayden Bartels and Jules LeBlanc are not dating, but are best friends.

The two 17-year-olds play the role of besties on screen and in real life.

Their show, Side Hustle, premiered on Nickelodeon in November of 2020.

On the show, the duo are tasked with finding a way to help pay for damages they made to their friend Munchy's family boat. The friends decide to create an app known as Kid-DING, which helps them connect with people looking for help with small or odd jobs.

According to Girls' Life, Jayden and Jules became even closer to each other during the nationwide quarantine in 2020 as a result of the coronavirus pandemic.

"I think all this has made us realize that we really need each other," Jayden said at the time.

"We've been going through a lot together."

In the interview, the two discuss how they often talk about boys, giving each other dating advice.

" I think [Jules'] ideal boy will always be someone who's nice to everyone and there for her no matter what," Jayden said, in conversation with Jules.

"Because you like when people are respectful, and you deserve that."

Who is Jayden Bartels?

Born on November 1, 2004, Jayden Bartels is an internet and television personality, actress, and singer.

On Youtube, Jayden has over 785K subscribers, and often uploads vlogs about her daily life or beauty routines.

She has appeared on Disney Channel's Coop & Cami Ask the World, as well as Nickelodeon's Side Hustle.

Jayden also made an appearance on Dance Moms, alongside the Ziegler sisters.

Her big break into fame came with the debut of TikTok, where she has over 8.8million followers and 1.6billion likes across her videos.

Known for singing and dancing with her friends, Jayden has also released original music.

Who is Jules LeBlanc?

Formerly known as Annie, Jules LeBlanc was born on December 5, 2004.

Her full name is Julianna, and as a child, her younger brother reportedly couldn't pronounce the name properly, nicknaming her Annie.

When she changed her name officially to Jules across her social media accounts in 2020, her fans reacted - some in confusion.

Jules took to her Instagram to clear the air, saying: "So a lot of you may know that I’m starting to go by Jules. My real name is Julianna, so I’m starting to go by Jules.

"I changed my TikTok, and surprisingly, a lot of people liked it, and I was not ready for it. I feel like for some reason people are mad that I’m doing it.”

While her family has been posting videos of Jules to Youtube since she was a four-year-old gymnast, the star has gone on to create an extensive following of her own.

She has over 4.4million followers on Youtube, along with 18.5million followers on TikTok and 9.5million followers on Instagram.
