Paris Fury enjoys epic family day out with Christmas shopping, sushi &amp; Five Guys - &amp; she shares cute

July 2024 · 3 minute read

BUSY mum-of-seven Paris Fury has revealed that she has finally started her Christmas shopping - with less than two weeks before the big day.

Yesterday, Paris enjoyed an epic day out with her family and has shared an insight into their shopping trip, complete with sushi, coffee AND Five Guys on social media.

Not only this, but wife of heavyweight champion Tyson Fury also shared an adorable video of her newborn son - Prince Rico Paris Fury.

Posting on her Instagram stories, Paris wrote: “Starting Christmas shopping December 13th is slightly stressful.”

But after a bit of shopping, Paris took a trip to Yo Sushi for a spot of lunch.

The blonde beauty enjoyed loaded fries and a chicken katsu curry, alongside a glass of fizz.

As Paris used chopsticks to tuck into her lunch, her oldest daughter Venezuela filmed the pair of them, as the mum-of-seven added: “Quick sushi stop with my girl.”

Seconds later, Paris shared an adorable video of her newborn son - Prince Rico Paris Fury - who was born on September 18, lying down in his pram. 

Wearing a Peter Rabbit outfit, Paris filmed her beautiful son in a store changing room, as he was visibly mesmerised by the shop’s lights.

As she panned the camera around to show off the lighting, we saw Paris’ large Michael Kors shopping bag, as she asked her son: “Are you looking at them?”

Shortly after, Paris shared a gorgeous selfie of her posing next to a floral-themed backdrop, as she drank a cup of coffee in River Island.

She shared: “Coffee while I wait.” 

To finish off the family day, Paris took a pit stop to Five Guys, where she got her hands on a delicious milkshake.

To end the busy day out, the mother confirmed: “Last stop.” 

Paris’ family day out comes shortly after her extravagant holiday to Saudi Arabia with husband Tyson.

The couple recently enjoyed some banter during their lavish trip, as the pair flew to the Middle Eastern nation via a private jet, for some quality time with their seven kids.

The Gypsy King and his family were seen enjoying the sun, the swimming pool and some water sports.

The Furys shared a series of photos from their holiday and Paris wasn't the only one that stole the show.

The same goes for their second youngest son, Prince Adonis Amaziah, as the family posed for a photo and the four-year-old could be seen pulling some cheeky antics.
