Sister Wives star Kody Brown is dating and still seeking a new wife despite insisting he will

October 2024 · 4 minute read

SISTER Wives’ Kody Brown is seeking a new wife despite insisting he will never marry again, The U.S. Sun has exclusively learned.

Kody is on the lookout for another lady to spiritually get married to after three of his four wives - Meri, Christine, and Janelle - split from him over the past two years.

A source told The U.S. Sun: "There is not a new sister wife just yet, but that doesn't mean Kody's not dating.

"He is actively dating and still seeking a new wife."

The source continued: "Within the next few months, fans are going to be hearing about Kody dating again.

"And his motive is really to please his [last-standing wife] Robyn, who still wants to be in a polygamous relationship and have a sister wife."

The U.S. Sun previously reported that Kody had filmed a courtship with a potential new wife, but it might not see the light of day since he was "humiliated" after being dumped.

Christine, 51, was the first sister wife to publicly announce her separation from Kody in November 2021 after 25 years of marriage.

After that, Janelle, 54, and Meri, 52, announced their splits during a Sister Wives special that aired in December 2022.

The U.S. Sun has reached out to TLC for comment.

ROAD TO REDEMPTION

Not only is Kody actively dating, but he's "actively looking to get back in the church and use his faith" as a guide.

The Browns are members of the Apostolic United Brethren, a sect of the Mormon religion. 

"He's working right now on redeeming himself after all the negative backlash.

"He wants to make things right again," the source added.

In a recent interview with People, Kody admitted he's looking to reconcile with at least one ex-wife, Janelle.

"I'm still looking for reconciliation with Janelle. I won’t say she’s been wishy-washy about the departure," he said.

"But I keep thinking, 'This isn’t a reason for us to break up. We can work through this. We can find trust again and negotiate an agreement on that trust if we’ll just work on it.'"

An insider believes Janelle "would take him back" if they ever get to that place.

He also told the outlet that after what he's been through, he has "no reason" to get married again - something the source denied.

The father of 18 explained: "The idea of ever marrying again after the experience I’ve had, there’s no reason for me to do that.

"I feel like I've hit the bottom of a deep swimming pool, but I've pushed off and am headed to the surface."

STAYING RELEVANT

Even though Kody has insisted he doesn't want to marry again, he might need a new wife to secure his stature on TV.

A source told The U.S. Sun that the current season, season 18, might be the last one after nearly 13 years on air.

"Christine's spinoff [Cooking With Just Christine] is a test to see fan reaction.

"TLC wants to see if people truly want to have a spinoff series on Christine and Janelle.

"If that's the case, then it's practically guaranteed that this will be the last season of Sister Wives," the source spilled.

However, the famous family's story can be saved if there is a new sister wife brought on, per the source.

"If he can find a new wife and keep his story going, there will be more Sister Wives seasons," the insider claimed.

"If not, it's going to come to an end and there will be a spinoff - or spinoffs - and the family will move on with their next adventure."

SPOILING ROBYN

After three wives left him, Kody is doing everything possible to make sure Robyn doesn't also leave him.

The couple, who officially tied the knot in 2014, has been seen in Las Vegas, Nevada multiple times in recent months, and it's all thanks to the patriarch.

"All of Kody's grand gestures and lavish trips and shopping extravaganzas are to make Robyn happy," a source confided.

"He's spending a lot of money on Robyn."

The U.S. Sun reported that the Arizona-based couple was having marital problems and were "not doing very well."

In Robyn's new interview, she hinted at their issues and said "it's been hell" and "very difficult" to move forward.

She also confessed that divorce "brings out the worst in you and sides of somebody you didn't know existed."

"I don't think anybody should be married while they're going through a divorce," Robyn admitted.
