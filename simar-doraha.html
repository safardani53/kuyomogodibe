Simar Doraha Biography

Simar Doraha was born in 1992 in Doraha, a city in the Indian state of Punjab. He is now residing in Mohali, Chandigarh. Mansimar Singh Bajwa is his given name.

He completed his secondary education at Shri Guru Nanak Sen. Sec. School in Doraha. Simar enrolled in S.C.D. Govt. College in Ludhiana to pursue his bachelor’s degree.

He aspired to attend Panjab University. He enrolled at Panjab University in 2017 and graduated with a Master’s degree in Psychology in 2019. He has also posted his grade sheets on his social media accounts.

Kultar Singh (father) and Inderjeet Kaur (mother) raised Mansimar Singh Bajwa (mother). So far, he has not revealed his family’s profession in the public domain.
