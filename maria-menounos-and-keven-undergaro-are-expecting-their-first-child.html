Maria Menounos And Keven Undergaro Are Expecting Their First Child

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Maria Menounos, a well-known journalist, TV personality, and actress, and her husband Keven Undergaro are expecting their first child. Their agent verified the news, and Maria also shared an ultrasound video on Instagram with Keven. Maria emphasizes in the video clip that it is her kid. Maria spoke to People about her pregnancy adventure, saying,

“After a decade of trying everything, we are very thankful to the wonderful family that assisted us in conceiving our kid. Keven, my father, and I are all very happy for this soul to enter our lives. “What a gift.”

Maria Menounos and Keven Undergaro married on New Year’s Eve in 2017, followed by another wedding the following year. The couple began dating in 1998 and got engaged in March 2016.

Keven Undergaro, Maria Menounos’ husband, is the originator of AfterBuzz TV.

Keven Undergaro, who is now 55 years old, just graduated from Saint Anselm College. He quickly became interested in creating a career in Hollywood. Undergaro began his career as a writer for the MTV dating game program Singled Out. He made an indie picture that was not well received and caused him financial difficulties. He chose to help Maria Menounos’ career after meeting her and learning all he could about the entertainment business.

Undergaro is well-known for co-creating AfterBuzz TV with his wife, Maria Menounos. They built the forum to talk about the AMC crime show Breaking Bad. AfterBuzz TV acquired a popularity for its after-show podcasts of various television programs. However, after the Covid-19 epidemic in 2020, the site is said to have stopped the creation of programs. Keven is also recognized for co-creating and executive producing the ABC program #DanceBattleAmerica. He is the writer and director of the dark comedy film Adventures of Serial Buddies, as well as the producer of Netflix’s This Isn’t Funny.

The journey of Maria Menounos and Keven Undergaro to become parents

Maria Menounos discussed her path to becoming a mother in an interview with People in February 2022. She said that she had not anticipated it taking so long and that she was becoming angrier with each passing day. Following Kim Kardashian’s advice, the couple selected surrogacy but were startled to find in 2021 that their first surrogate did not match medically. Maria stated,

“My fertility doctor recommended a mock cycle since we only had two very healthy embryos and want two children. The uterine lining would not develop to the point where it could be safely implanted and the embryo would adhere. We would have probably lost our only shot if we hadn’t done [a mimic cycle].”

Maria was concerned when the couple sought another surrogate. She even addressed her mother’s death, stating she is sorry that her mother would not be able to see her grandkids. According to Menounos, she told her mother that she and Keven were planning to have a baby. She added that when her mother died, she attempted to discover her life’s purpose and was content to lose herself in order to care for her mother.
