How many kids does rapper Moneybagg Yo have? The US Sun

September 2024 · 2 minute read

MONEYBAGG Yo is a rising rapper who is believed to be the father of eight children.

He explained in an 2017 interview that he had impregnated four women.

The 29-year-old Memphis native is currently dating model and socialite Ari Fletcher.

Who is Moneybagg Yo?

Moneybagg Yo is a rapper from Memphis, Tennessee.

He signed to Yo Gotti's label, Collective Music Group, in 2016.

He's best known for his hits All Dat, Said Sum, and Me vs Me.

Moneybagg, real name Demario DeWayne White, Jr dropped his Time Served project in January of this year.

It peaked at number 3 on the Billboard charts.

He was previously arrested in 2016 on weapons and drugs charges.

Who has he dated?

Moneybagg Yo briefly dated female rapper Megan Thee Stallion in 2019.

He is now dating Instagram model and socialite Ari Fletcher.

On her birthday on July 11, he gifted his new leading lady a Lamborghini Urus SUV truck at her decked-out birthday party.

The generous gesture got fans talking on social media and subsequently led to an exchange of works between Ari and one of Moneybagg Yo's children's mothers.

How many kids does he have?

It is believed that Moneybagg Yo has eight kids.

In 2017, Moneybagg Yo told Shade 45 that he had seven kids and four baby mothers.

He went on to say that he had two or three children by the time he was in 12th grade in high school.

He explained to the radio hosts that he was cordial with the mothers of his kids.

Since the interview, he has had at least one more baby - a little girl named Spring.

What is his net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Moneybagg Yo is estimated to be worth $1-1.5million.

Moneybagg Yo has been rapping since 2012 and has released three albums.
