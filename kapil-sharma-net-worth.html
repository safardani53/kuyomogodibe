INDIAN comedy icon Kapil Sharma has amassed an eye-watering net worth over the years.

Who is Kapil Sharma?

Kapil Sharma was born on April 2, 1981, in the Punjab region of India.

The successful comedian is best known for hosting his stand-up series, The Kapil Sharma Show.

He interviews celebrity guests on the popular chat show, as fans across the country tune in.

The show has been running since 2016.

Kapil, also called the Laughter King, shot to fame in 2007 when he was crowned victorious on reality TV show The Great Indian Laughter Challenge.

The television personality has presented several hit shows including Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa season six, and Chhote Miyan.

Kapil also participated as a much-loved contestant in Star Ya Rockstar.

He regularly appeared on the Comedy Circus shows from 2010 until 2013.

He has also been known to appear in the Bigg Boss house to entertain the contestants in a comedy night.

After winning Indian of the Year in the entertainment category of CNN-IBN's Indian of the Year awards, Kapil quickly made his Bollywood debut.

He has also spent the year on tour with his comedy show Kapil Sharma Live across the UK, USA, Canada, and Australia.

Is Kapil Sharma married and does he have any children?

Kapil tied the knot with his wife Ginni Chatrath on December 12, 2018, and the happy couple share two children together - a daughter and a son.

Their little girl, Anayra, was born in December 2019, a year after the pair married.

On February 1, 2021, baby boy Trishaan arrived, with Kapil posting online that "by the grace of God Baby n Mother both r fine".

Ginna was born on November 18, 1989, in Jalandhar, Punjab.

Comedy legend Kapil has been open about his struggles with mental health and addiction issues in the past, and credits Ginni to his recovery.

Despite controversies surrounding his career, his wife stood by his side and even helps manage his social media.
