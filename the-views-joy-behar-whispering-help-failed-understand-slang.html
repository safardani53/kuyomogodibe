THE VIEW host Joy Behar has been caught asking a co-host for help during the broadcast after failing to understand a slang word.

The live TV moment occurred during the cooking segment of the show featuring celebrity chef and restauranteur Guy Fieri.

Guy, 55, joined The View co-hosts Joy Behar, 80, Sunny Hostin, 54, Sara Haines, 45, and Alyssa Farah Griffin, 33, during the extended segment to prepare a "buffet" of tasty entries for the panel to sample.

The chef answered questions about the upcoming fourth season of Food Network's Tournament of Champions as he prepared barbeque shotgun shells and a birria quesadilla pizza.

Later during the interview, Sunny asked Guy about the new car he purchased for his son Ryder for his 17th birthday.

The morning show moderator revealed that she recently bought her daughter Paloma a new car as well.

FREUDIAN FAUX PAS

Earlier in the show, Joy was been left red-faced after making a major NSFW slip-up during a serious news segment.

She accidentally said "erection" instead of "insurrection" during a politics recap on Wednesday's show.

Joy was discussing the controversial news that Fox News host Tucker Carlson was given footage from the Capitol riot.

She said: "Less than a week after Tucker Carlson is caught fully admitting that he doesn't believe the election fraud lies he's putting on the air, House Speaker Kevin McCarthy hands him capital security footage from the January 6 erection-"

Joy broke off in shock and tried to hastily correct herself by saying, "election," before finally settling on the correct "insurrection."

The comedian covered her mouth with her hand in embarrassment and hung her head as her co-hosts burst out laughing.

Alyssa jokingly stated: "Well done!"

Sara and Sunny, meanwhile, tried to hide their faces and pursed their lips with barely suppressed laughter.

Fans of The View went wild over Joy's gaffe - especially as she was acting as show moderator during Whoopi Goldberg's absence.

One fan tweeted, "Erection, Joy?" and added a meme with the text, "Something on your mind?"

Another posted: "Joy Behar with the best Freudian slip ever!"

A third commented, "Erection, election, insurrection," with a string of laughing face emojis.
