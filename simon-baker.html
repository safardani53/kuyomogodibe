Charity Works

Simon Baker has been involved in various charitable endeavors throughout his career. Here are a few examples of his charitable work:

  • The Butterfly Foundation: Baker has been an ambassador for The Butterfly Foundation, an Australian organization that supports people with eating disorders and body image issues.

  • Stand Up To Cancer: Baker participated in the Stand Up To Cancer telethon in 2008, which aimed to raise funds for cancer research and treatment.

  • R U OK?: He has also supported the R U OK? campaign, an Australian suicide prevention initiative that encourages people to have meaningful conversations and check in on the mental well-being of others.

  • Children's Hospital Los Angeles: Baker has been involved with fundraising efforts for the Children's Hospital Los Angeles, which provides healthcare services to children in need.

  • AIDS/LifeCycle: He has participated in the AIDS/LifeCycle event, a seven-day bike ride that raises funds and awareness for HIV/AIDS-related services.
