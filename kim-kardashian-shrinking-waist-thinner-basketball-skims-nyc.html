KIM Kardashian has alarmed critics with her slim appearance at a recent public appearance.

The Skims founder posed next to two officials from the NBA.

Kim showed off her thin figure in a pink Chanel coat and black Skims leggings paired with pointed black booties.

The shapewear brand has expanded into briefs, undershirts, and boxers.
