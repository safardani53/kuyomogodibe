Anna Loginova Killed In Porsche Carjack (Photos)

July 2024 · 1 minute read

Russia’s most famous and gorgeous celebrity bodyguard, Anna Loginova, was killed while attempting to prevent a carjacker from stealing her Porsche. The beautiful 29 year old clung to the car door handle and died of head injuries as she was dragged along a roadway at high speed.

Loginova, highly respected for her security skills, worked for wealthy, high profile Russian clients. She ran a female protection agency to provide security for Moscow’s billionaires as well as their wives and children.

In a previous career, Anna Loginova did ad campaigns for BMW, Chanel and other world-known brands. Collaborating with BMW, Loginova took a course of extreme driving. Afterward, she met a man who owned an economic security firm and subsequently became a bodyguard, eventually starting her own business. She posed for Maxim magazine in December 2007 to remind her fellow Russian’s that not only is she good with a gun but still has a feminine side.

According to Daily Mail, carjacking is common in Moscow, where 50 Porsche’s have been stolen in the past year, 12 within the last two months.
