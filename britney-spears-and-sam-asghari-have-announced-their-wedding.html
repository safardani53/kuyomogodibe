Britney Spears And Sam Asghari Have Announced Their Wedding

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Britney Spears and Sam Asghari have tied the knot. They met on the set of the music video for Spears’ 2016 album, Slumber Party, and reunited five months later. Brandon Cohen, the latter’s publicist, confirmed the wedding news, noting that Asghari has desired it for a long time. Spears also announced the news on her Instagram account on Friday:

Spears also said that they fell to the dance floor twice, and she posted a few images from the occasion, including one of her wearing a long veil, riding in a horse-drawn carriage, and kissing Asghari on a balcony. Donatella Versace created Spears’ gown, and she said on Instagram that Britney has always had a particular place in her heart.

A look at Britney Spears and Sam Asghari’s dating history.

Britney Spears and Sam Asghari met on the shoot of Spears’ Slumber Party music video and were romantically involved. She claimed in a 2017 interview that it took her and Asghari around five months to reconcile after the video filming. The couple dated for five years before announcing their engagement in September 2021, only one month before Britney’s conservatorship ended. Spears also revealed her wedding-day outfit and presented her new kitten, Wendy, to fans last month.

According to a source, Spears had been arranging her wedding for almost nine months after her engagement. On his Instagram story, Asghari also uploaded a flashback picture of Spears and him kissing, in which Britney raised up her ring finger to show off her gold.

More information about Britney Spears and Sam Asghari’s wedding

Britney Spears married Sam Asghari on Thursday night in Los Angeles. Around 60 people attended the event. Spears was seen wearing a gown from the well-known design company Versace. Donatella Versace also revealed a video of Spears’ wedding gown as well as drawings of her creating it. She also shared some photos and a video of herself with Spears, as well as Kathy Hilton, Madonna, Selena Gomez, and Drew Barrymore. Can’t Help Falling in Love by Elvis Presley played as Spears went down the aisle. She changed into a short black gown after the wedding and spent the night dancing with her husband and close friends.


At the 2003 MTV Video Music Awards, Spears and Madonna reprised their memorable kiss. Aerial photographs from the wedding show a big tent being erected in the singer’s garden. Britney’s divorced parents, Jamie and Lynne Spears, as well as her sister Jamie Lynn Spears, did not attend the wedding due to their disagreement with her over her conservatorship, which expires in November 2021. Despite being invited, her brother Bryan Spears did not attend the wedding. Britney and Sam had a ride in a Rolls Royce while holding champagne glasses. Spears flaunted her diamonds and bridal-themed manicure, calling the look “refined.”
