When is Hospital on BBC Two tonight, where in London is St Mary's what is the documentary about?

August 2024 · 2 minute read

HOSPITAL continues on BBC Two tonight as the cameras return to St Mary's to follow the staff as they deal with some tricky medical cases.

Here's all the info as the BBC documentary airs the final episode of its second series...

What is Hospital and when is it on?

The fourth and final episode of the new series airs on BBC Two at 9pm tonight (July 11)

Hospital is a BBC documentary series that takes cameras inside a busy London hospital.

It shows doctors and nurses at work and charts the difficult decisions they are faced with when dealing with patients coming into the A&E department.

Tonight's episode goes head-to-head with In The Dak on BBC One, Midsomer Murders on ITV, Phil Spencer: Find Me A Home on Channel 4 and The Hotel Inspector on Channel 5.

If you miss an episode you can catch up on BBC iPlayer shortly after broadcast.

What hospital is the BBC documentary Hospital based in?

The BBC Two documentary series Hospital is filmed in St Mary’s Hospital in Paddington, London.

The hospital is one of London’s four Major Trauma Centres with staff trained to deal with major incidents.

St Mary's was founded in 1845 and several members of the Royal Family have been born there including Princess Charlotte and Prince George.

Does the Westminster Bridge terror attack feature in the BBC documentary series Hospital?

The camera crews of the acclaimed BBC show Hospital began filming the second series on Wednesday 22 March 2017.

This was the day that terrorist Khalid Masood purposely drove a car into pedestrians on Westminster Bridge in London before being shot by police while attempting to storm Westminster palace.

The camera crews capture the moment Khalid is brought to the hospital under armed guard before being declared dead, and sees victims of the fanatic being treated by the heroic hospital staff.
