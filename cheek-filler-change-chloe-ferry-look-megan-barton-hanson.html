March 2024 · 3 minute read

FROM lip filller to bum implants and botox, there are a range of treatments that are known for being popular with the celebs.

The chipmunk cheek is becoming the latest ‘tweakment’ that stars are all demanding.

Celebrities that have gotten cheek filler before and after

Chloe Ferry

The Geordie Shore star, 27, is one of the most vocal celebrities when it comes to surgery.

Chloe told The Sun’s Fabulous Magazine in 2021: “I love surgery. I've had loads of fillers in my face and a nose job, but I still don't like my nose so I want another one."

To make her jaw "pop" she had fillers injected into her cheeks and chin, and even admitted that she wanted muscles removed from her face to give her a sharp appearance.

Megan Barton Hanson

In January 2022, Love Island star Megan said she is quitting cosmetic surgery aside from botox and fillers.

The reality star said therapy has helped her to finally be happy with her body and that she's in the "most content place she's ever been".

She said: "I’ve learned that surgery is no substitute for working on your mental health or self-esteem," she wrote in Grazia Magazine.

Amy Childs

The former Towie Star started having surgery at a young age - which slowly turned into an addiction.

But in 2022, Amy admitted that she “wished she’d never had fillers”.

Opening up to OK! Magazine, she said: "In terms of filler, changing my face and that, I'm very happy in myself.

"I can't even remember the last time I had Botox done!"

Courtney Cox

Courtney is another celeb to regret her fillers.

The Friends star had many of her fillers dissolved in 2017, admitting that she “didn’t look like herself.”

She said: "I think that I now look more like the person that I was. I hope I do.

"Things are going to change. Everything’s going to drop. I was trying to make it not drop, but that made me look fake.

"You need movement in your face, especially if you have thin skin like I do.

"Those aren’t wrinkles—they’re smile lines. I’ve had to learn to embrace movement and realize that fillers are not my friend."

Kylie Minogue

Kylie confessed to experimenting with Botox and fillers in the past.

The pop princess said: “I’m preferring to be a lot more natural these days. I’ve tried Botox, I’m definitely not one of those people who says, ‘You shouldn’t do this’.”
