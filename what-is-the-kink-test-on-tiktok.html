What is the Kink Test on TikTok?

April 2024 · 2 minute read

THE Kink Test is a brand new quiz that is trending on TikTok right now, and you’ve probably guessed, it’s all about how adventurous you are.

Here’s everything you need to know about the test and how you can take it...

What is the Kink Test on Tiktok?

The Kink Test is a quiz that tells you how physically adventurous you are.

It was created in 2014 to tell people what kink would best fit their physical experience, but only now has it become popular with users on TikTok revealing their results. 

There are two tests to choose from, either the long or short one.

The longer quiz is for people who really care about BDSM and are interested in it. 

How do I take the Kink Test? 

Firstly, you will need to fill out a little bit of information about yourself such as your name, age, sexual orientation and language.

Then you will have the option of either a long or short test.

Once you've decided, you will be shown a series of statements which you have to either agree or disagree with.
