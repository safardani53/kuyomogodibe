July 2024 · 4 minute read

TORI Spelling is afraid husband Dean McDermott may cheat again as he spends six months filming a new TV show in Canada, The Sun can reveal.

The former Beverly Hills 90210 star has been telling pals of her fears the actor may be unfaithful again while he is living away from home filming cop drama Lady Dicks in Canada.

A source exclusively told The Sun: "Tori has always worried about infidelity and now that Dean has left to work outside the US she is living in fear he might just cheat again.

"When Tori got the news Dean had got an acting role she was proud of him and also thrilled that he would contribute to the family income.

"But in the back of her mind she couldn't help get that feeling she tries so hard to suppress, the fear he would meet another woman.

"Dean's new acting gig is a new police series and he has to spend six months away shooting in Toronto."

The insider continued: "It's hard because she loves him but Toronto is where he got caught cheating before and it brings up all sorts of images."

The source also explained how difficult it's been for the actress because of the current coronavirus pandemic.

The insider said: "Covid hasn't made this any easier for Tori because she has all the kids at home and going to Canada with five kids during this time is close to impossible.

"Everyone would have to quarantine and Americans aren't even allowed in Canada at the moment unless you are a citizen or have a family emergency.

"Tori would love to get a babysitter for all the kids but that's also tough at this time.

"She is just going to have to do her best and even with all their couples therapy that is no easy feat."

Tori, 47, and Dean, 53, got married in 2006 and have five children Liam, 12, Stella, 11, Hattie, seven, Finn, six, and Beau, two.

In 2013, US Weekly reported that Dean cheated with a woman named Emily Goodhand while they were in Canada promoting his show Chopped Canada.

Emily told the publication that she only agreed to sleep with Dean after he told her he was in a sexless marriage.

Soon afterwards Dean checked into rehab for sex addiction.

Dean opened up about his decision to cheat earlier in his new podcast Daddy Issues, where he admitted: “I really, really wish that I had been given the knowledge of thinking with your head, not your penis, because I did a lot of thinking with my penis."

He added: “It’s so not about the person you’re with. … For me, it wasn’t about T. It was about inadequacies in myself, I felt so bad about myself, I felt like a piece of s**t.

"I needed to do that to feel some kind of power. It was horrible and I’m an a**hole, piece of s**t for doing it, [but] our relationship is better than ever. That was a rough Christmas, we came out the other end.”

Speaking on Roxy Manning and Tammin Sursok's podcast Women On Top, Tori also spoke about how she always feared Dean would cheat because she had no self-confidence.

"It was a hard thing to navigate," she said, adding that she "loves him" and "adores him" and that "after 14 years I still look at him and think he's so hot."
