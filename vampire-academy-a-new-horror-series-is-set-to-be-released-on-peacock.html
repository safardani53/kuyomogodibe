Vampire Academy A New Horror Series Is Set To Be Released On Peacock

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Vampire Academy, Peacock’s latest fantasy horror drama, will debut on the platform on September 15, 2022. The series, a reworking of Mark Waters’ film of the same name, revolves around the lives of two friends, Rose Hathaway and Lissa Dragomir, who attend St. Vladimir’s Academy. The show’s principal actors are Sisi Stringer and Daniela Nieves, with many more playing important supporting parts. Continue reading to discover when Vampire Academy will be available on Peacock, see the trailer, and learn more.

Vampire Academy Peacock release date, narrative, what to anticipate, and more

Vampire Academy is set to debut on Peacock on September 15, 2022, at 5:00 a.m. ET (tentative). The first four episodes of the program will be published on the same day, and the remaining six episodes will be distributed on a weekly basis. On August 17, 2022, Peacock released the official teaser for the program, which provides a look at the drama’s gorgeous, enigmatic universe filled with interesting individuals.

The dramatic video teases a number of exciting events that will take place in the new season without giving away too many specifics that may ruin the watching experience. Overall, the teaser develops a unique spooky tone that gothic horror lovers will certainly like. Along with the trailer, Peacock released the following summary for the show:

“In a world of opulence and splendor, the relationship of two young ladies overcomes their drastically different classes as they prepare to finish their schooling and join vampire society. One as a mighty Royal, the other as a half-vampire Guardian trained to guard their community from the vicious ‘Strigoi.’ If Royal infighting doesn’t get the job done first.”

Viewers should expect a captivating and exciting horror series that merges fantasy and gothic horror themes to create a remarkable cinematic experience based on the teaser and description.

A short peek at the cast of Vampire Academy

Rosemary Hathaway is played by Sisi Stringer in Vampire Academy. Stringer had a fantastic performance in the teaser, exuding charm and confidence in her part. Viewers should expect a somber performance from the actress. Stringer is most known for her work on Carnifex, Mortal Kombat, and Bloody Hell, in addition to Vampire Academy.

Daniela Nieves, who plays Vasilisa Dragomir opposite Stringer in another crucial role, looks similarly excellent in the trailer. Daniela’s relationship with Stringer is crucial in creating the tone for the series. Nieves is most known for her appearances on the Nickelodeon series Every Witch Way. She’s also featured in Five Points, WITS Academy, and a variety of other publications. Aside from the performers named above, the film contains a slew of others in supporting roles:

  • Christian Ozera is played by André Dae Kim.
  • Dimitri Belikov is played by Kieron Moore.
  • Victor Dashkov is played by J August Richards.
  • Mia McKenna-Bruce in the role of Mia Karp
  • Tatiana Vogel is played by Anita-Joy Uwajeh.
