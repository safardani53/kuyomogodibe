Jack Grealish's girlfriend Sasha Attwood signs up to A-list agency Elite Models

May 2024 · 9 minute read

HER relationship with footie ace Jack Grealish might have been on blast lately, but Sasha Attwood is putting her best foot forward by signing up to A-list agency Elite Models.

The stunning blonde has kept a dignified silence after her Manchester City and England star fella was recently linked to both Emily Atack and Amber Gill.

But she has been busy focusing on her career.

The fashionista said: “I am so excited to be working with Elite Model Management.

“I’ve been modelling since I was 12 years old and I can’t wait for this next chapter of my career.” 

It comes as Love Island star Maura Higgins last week inked a similar deal with the agency, which includes a US arm.

Glee-Anne eyes up solo deal

THEY’VE spent a decade topping the charts, now the Little Mix girls look increasingly like they are going their separate ways.

We can reveal Leigh-Anne Pinnock is the latest to eye up a solo career amid speculation the group will split next year – and is in talks with a top record label.

Our sister column Bizarre revealed yesterday that her band-mate Jade Thirlwall was in top-secret talks with record label Atlantic.

 Now sources say Leigh-Anne, who will co-host the Mobo Awards in Coventry next month, is “thrilled” to be close to landing her own lucrative record deal.

 A source last night said: “This is really exciting for Leigh-Anne. She has lots of ideas about what she would like to achieve as a solo artist.

“The team she is talking to have been very supportive of those ambitions, and she is now really looking forward to working with them.

“It looks set to be a great partnership, and she is thrilled.”

Leigh-Anne’s talks come after she hired Dua Lipa’s management firm Tap to represent her.

The Sun revealed in October that Leigh-Anne, Jade and Perrie Edwards will pursue solo careers after Little Mix’s much-anticipated 2022 tour. 

Sources told at the time how the girls were on the brink of announcing their split.

Alongside her music ambitions, Leigh-Anne, who welcomed twins with her fiance, footballer Andre Gray in August, is also pursuing a movie career after landing a role in festive flick Boxing Day, which is released next month.

Meanwhile Perrie – who also became a mum to baby Axel with Liverpool’s Alex Oxslade-Chamberlain in August – is carving out a role as a lifestyle expert. 

She recently launched her own clothing brand, Disora, and is also likely to forge a solo singing career with Sony keen to snap her up.

It’s great to know the girls are poised to storm the charts next year – even if it isn’t together.

Anastacia: Let's show our scars

ANASTACIA has bravely opened up about her scars, saying she hopes she gives young fans courage.

The singer, who has previously revealed she has scars from a double mastectomy and another on her stomach from a Crohn’s disease op, has revealed she recently had surgery on two herniated discs in her neck, despite her fears that it could harm her voice.

She said: “It’s important for fans to see their idols with scars because there is such a filter now and need for perfection in this world.

“Earlier this year I had surgery on my neck after I went hang-gliding in New Zealand and I really messed up both my herniated discs to the point that surgery was the only option.

“I don’t mind scars. I don’t want them necessarily. I’m not overthinking it now. I’d rather be healthy and pain-free.”

Anastacia is looking forward to her upcoming I’m Outta Lockdown European tour and said: “I’m really excited. We’re going to have some fun.” 

The singer, who was married to her bodyguard Wayne Newton for three years until 2010, spent lockdown with her mother and brother and is currently single.

She said: “I’m not dating anyone. I’m dating myself at the moment because of the ’rona. It’s been a little hard to get on the dating scene since I would have to put somebody in jail.

“I’m a germaphobe – I’m not dating until we see what is going on with this situation, but it is very difficult.”

Sex Education is adding to the cast

BOSSES of Netflix’s Sex Education are searching for two new cast members after adding a pair of transgender characters.

Filming for the fourth season of the streaming hit, which stars Gillian Anderson, Asa Butterfield and Emma Mackey, begins in April.

And casting chiefs want youngsters for the new roles of Abbi and Kent.

Abbi is described as “a young trans woman with a Nineties WINONA RYDER vibe, who is confident and self-assured in her gender identity”. Meanwhile, boyfriend Kent is “trans-masculine, goofy, forgetful and a great listener”.

The casting call specifies that no acting experience is necessary.

Season three introduced Cal (musician DUA SALEH), a non-binary student from Minneapolis.

Dua praised the team for presenting a nuanced non-binary individual, saying: “I don’t see many non-binary characters depicted in a way that are authentic and true to the whole person.

“It didn’t feel like Cal was a caricature.

“Knowing a role like this is so remarkable is making me reflect a lot – and have a lot of gratitude for where the world is going. Just being ourselves...it feels amazing.”
