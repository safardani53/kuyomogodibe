Who is Lil Durk's fiance, India Royale?

September 2024 · 3 minute read

LIL Durk proposed to India Royale in December 2021, but unconfirmed rumors are circulating that the couple has broken up.

The pair's relationship has been on and off since they first started dating in 2017.

Who is India Royale?

India Royale is known for her relationship and engagement to Lil Durk, whose legal name is Durk Derrick Banks.

Royale is a model and Instagram influencer from Chicago, Illinois where she grew up with her three older siblings.

She has amassed over 4.3million followers on Instagram and has a YouTube channel with 486k subscribers.

The mother-of-two also runs her own beauty businesses - India Royale Beauty and India Royale’s Hair Vendor.

When did the couple start dating and how many kids do they have?

Lil Durk and Royale started dating in 2017 and he proposed to her on stage at his concert in Chicago in December 2021.

He stood in front of a watching crowd and thanked Royale for being "the realest" and said: "You know I love you to death.

“You’ve been holding me down when I was going through a lot. You’re the realest and I just love you to death."

He then asked Royale: "Do you want to be my wife?"

The crowd began cheering as Royale said: "Yes."

Lil Durk discussed why he cares about his fiancé on Gillie The King and Wallo's Million Dollaz Worth Of Game podcast in March 2022.

He was asked on the podcast what attracted him to Royale, and he responded that is was her low "body count," authenticity, and loyalty.

“I’m petty as hell,” Lil Durk said, adding: “Like, if you f***ed a lot of n****s and all that, I ain’t gonna wife you. But that’s me.

"... But it’s, like, everything about her. Being real, not cheating, she ain’t been passed around. It was, like, her body count. That’s what really did it.”

Criticism of Lil Durk's comments quickly sprung up on social media with one person writing on Twitter: "Imagine ur fiancé being asked what he love about u & the first thing he brings up is ur body count etc.

"Not your personality, not your loyalty, not your sacrifices to him but your past relations.

"Hearing this from my man would not turn me on at all esp when his past is opposite."

However, Royale responded in a separate tweet, writing: "Idgaf what people think about my fiancé. That’s why he mine, get you one.”

Do India Royale and Lil Durk have children?

Royale gave birth to her and Lil Durk's first child together, Willow Banks, in 2018.

Lil Durk has also fathered five other children in addition to Willow, and welcomed his first child Angelo when he was 17 years old.

Royale also has one other child from a previous relationship and has said she and Lil Durk had talked about having one more baby.

"I want my next two kids back to back. Then I’m done," she tweeted. 

"He would love that. He was never more obsessed with me when I was pregnant. Like on me, on me."

Did India Royale and Lil Durk break up?

Rumors that Royale and Lil Durk have broken up surfaced after she dropped hints on her social media platforms.

In one tweet, Royale wrote: "I'm a free agent," prompting responses questioning if her and Lil Durk have broken up.

"If you and durk ain’t together no more imma stop dating cause y’all were the only hope I had left in love," one person commented.
