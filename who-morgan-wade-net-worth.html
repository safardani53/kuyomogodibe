MORGAN Wade is widely known for her hit country songs Wilder Days and Take Me Away.

As the country artist continues to succeed, fans want to know more about her life.

Who is Morgan Wade?

Morgan Dealle Wade is an up-and-coming country singer born December 10, 1994, in Floyd, Virginia.

In 2018, Wade took to Craigslist to scout musicians who would help her record her first album, Puppets with My Heart.

Wade later went on to release her hit album, Reckless, with the record label Thirty Tigers, in 2021.

The same year, her song Wilder Days was debuted as her first single from her studio album.

The Virginia native went on to re-release her debut studio album via Arista Nashville with six additional songs including a cover of Elvis Presley's Suspicious Minds.

What is her net worth?

According to multiple outlets, Morgan Wade has a net worth ranging from $5million to $7million.

Apart from Puppets with My Heart and Reckless, Wade is set to release her third album, Psychopath, on August 25, 2023.

Wade is embarking on a 45-date world tour to promote her new album.

How do Morgan Wade and Kyle Richards know each other?

As Morgan Wade continues to grow her fandom, she has gained the attention of RHOBH star, Kyle Richards.

In February 2022, Richards posted a picture of herself, Wade and some RHOBH friends via Instagram.

The accompanied caption read: "I stalked @morganwademusic on IG after listening to her music while driving solo from Utah to Colorado while making #housewivesofthenorthpole & today we met up in person for the 1st time.

"The most unlikely of friendships some may think but kinship knows no boundaries."

Wade commented on the post saying: "Y'all are the best. So glad you stalked me."

Their friendship has been seen to have grown throughout the year.
