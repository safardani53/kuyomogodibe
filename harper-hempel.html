Career And Profession

High School Career

Harper was a starter for her high school volleyball team for four years. During her high school volleyball career, the Kentucky Volleyball Coaches Association (KVCA) named Hempel to the All-State for three-time. 

Likewise, she won the district MVP honors during her senior season and was also voted as the team captain by her teammates three times.

She also earned the Team Leadership Award 2011 for her exceptional leadership qualities.

Moreover, Hempel won the Raider Award, given to the student with top performance on and off-court for two straight years.

College Career

Harper represented the team Wildcats of the University of Kentucky. She joined the team as a defensive specialist and setter.

During her sophomore season, Harper played in 13 matches and finished the season with a kill, an ace, 20 digs, and three assists.

She recorded the top 5 digs of the season while playing against Illinois and Wichita State.

Similarly, Harper recorded the first kill of her career while playing against Wichita State, and while playing against Northern Illinois, she registered her first career ace.

During her junior year, Harper played in 17 matches and recorded a career-high of five assists while playing against Marquette University.

She became one of the six players with the 25-point rally score at Kentucky to have 45+ assists in Kentucky.

Additionally, she recorded a career-high of five digs while playing against the Golden Eagles and tallied an ace playing against Arkansas.

In her senior year, Harper appeared in 13 matches and recorded a match-winning ace against Louisiana State University.

Marketing Career

Hempel has always had an interest in marketing. She also graduated from college with a Bachelor’s degree in Marketing and Digital Media.

Harper is a social media community manager for Fact & Fiction. There she takes care of community management and social management services for famous companies like Voodoo Ranger and Halo Top Ice Cream.

Moreover, Hempel works as a social media marketing manager for All Social Jessie. She directs and carries out content calendars as an independent consultant for patrons’ social media platforms.
