Aldis magical Christmas decorations range goes on sale TODAY and prices start at 2.99 Th

July 2024 · 2 minute read

ALDI is launching its range of festive decorations tomorrow, and the supermarket promises to "make it the brightest Christmas ever".

The collection includes indoor and outdoor lights, as well as novelty decorations, and prices start at just 89p for tinsel, and £2.99 for tree decorations.

As with all Aldi Specialbuys, the items are limited-edition and once they're gone, they're gone.

Among the stand-out pieces is a light-up stag's head, costing £29.99, which is battery operated and promises to "add a fun festive ambience to any setting."

The stag head is £20 cheaper than a lookalike item by John Lewis, which retails for £50.

Aldi is also selling a 6ft inflatable snowman, costing £19.99, which is £15 cheaper than Homebase's version.

There's also an inflatable Santa, polar bear and stop sign on offer - for the same price - or shoppers can pick up a light-up glitter tree for £34.99.

Prices start at 89p for a two-metre strip of tinsel, while the supermarket is also selling a range of novelty baubles priced at £2.99 for a set of three.

While shoppers can also buy a Christmas wreath or garland for £9.99, and a light-up Christmas snowman, Santa or penguin for £7.99.

If you like the look of these, you may also want to check out B&M's Christmas range.

The budget shop is selling a Christmas nutcracker decoration for £29.99 - making it £50 cheaper than the John Lewis version.

And B&M is also selling a light-up reindeer for £14.99 - saving customers £55 compared to Debenhams' version.
